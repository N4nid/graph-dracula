User Interface

//ui-manual

This is the general UI layout, you are gonna see, when you open the application.

Global Menu

On the top left, you can see the global menu, which has submenus for file and help.
- Under file, the is the option to quit the application
  But be carefull, you will loose all your changes.
- Under help, you will find the options to open the user manual or the about window.

Graph View

Below the global menu, you will find the big coordinate system, which is your graph view.
This view always displays a labled coordiante system and all the corresponding graphs
to your inputted functions, equations or parametric functions. (More about that under Text Input)
- You can move the view around to see different parts of your equations
  by clicking and holding the left mouse button.
- You can zoom in by scrolling the mouse wheel
- You can recenter or reset the zoom of the graph view by choosing the corresponding option
  under the menu, that appears when right clicking the Graph View.

Equation List

On the right side of the Graph View, you can see the Equation List. In this list,
all equations, that you have inputted, are gonna be listed, like in this screenshot:

- You can scroll through the list using you mouse wheel
- You can right click any listed equation to edit, hide or delete it.
- You can change the color of an equation's graph with the color picker
  by clicking the colored circle next to it an choosing a new color.

Custom Variables

If you use an unknown custom variable in your inputted equation, such as "a", they get automatically added
in a seperate list, like in the following example:

Custom variables have a single letter name and a value.
- You can change the value of a custom variable by either
  using the slider or typing the value into the provided text field, where it is displayed.
- Confirm a typed input by pressing enter or clicking somewhere else in the window.
- The range for the slider can be easily adjsuted by either inputting new values
  into the minnimum and maximum text fields at the left and the right side of the slider
  or by typing in a value outside of the current range into the value text field.
To delete an eqaution, just right click and press delete.

Equation Input

On the buttom of the page, there is a text field, where you can type in functions, equations
parametric functions and if-conditions. To learn more about how to do that, look at the section for text input.
- To add an input, press enter or click the plus button on the right
- To change the color of the input graph, click the colored circle on the right
  to open the color picker
- When you click the up-arrow on the left or press it's shortcut CTRL + E, you open the expand menu.

This menu makes it much easier to type in equations by providing templates for common functionality
like templates for functions, parametric funtions or if-conditions, special functions like sin(), or constants like Ï€.
Just click on the corresponding button to input it into the text field.
To close the expand menu, just click the button again press the shortcut again or click anywhere not related to the expand menu.
